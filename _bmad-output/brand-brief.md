# Karamania Brand Brief

## 1. What Is Karamania?

A real-time mobile karaoke party app where one person hosts and friends join via a code. An AI DJ runs the show — picking songs, dealing party cards, running ceremonies, and awarding crowd favorites. It's the energy of a karaoke night, gamified and in your pocket.

## 2. Brand Personality

**If Karamania were a person, they'd be:**
- The friend who grabs the mic first and gets everyone else to join
- Confident but not intimidating — welcoming to shy singers and showoffs alike
- A little dramatic (ceremonies! awards!), a lot of fun, never pretentious

**Three words:** **Bold. Playful. Electric.**

**NOT:** corporate, minimalist, cute, vintage, serious

## 3. Target Audience

- Groups of friends (3-12 people) at karaoke nights, house parties, gatherings
- Ages 18-35, social, phone-native
- They care about the *experience* more than the tech — this is a party enhancer, not a utility

## 4. Emotional Arc

The brand should support the emotional journey of a Karamania session:

| Moment | Emotion | Brand Energy |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Opening the app | Excitement, anticipation | Bold, inviting |
| Lobby / waiting | Building energy, social | Warm, connected |
| Song performance | Peak fun, spotlight | Electric, vibrant |
| Ceremony / awards | Celebration, recognition | Dramatic, rewarding |
| Session recap | Nostalgia, sharing | Warm, proud |

## 5. Visual Direction

### 5.1 Logo Requirements

The logo needs to work in these contexts:
- **App icon** (60x60px, rounded corners) — must be recognizable at small size
- **Home screen wordmark** — hero placement on dark background
- **QR code center overlay** — small mark inside the lobby QR code
- **Light AND dark backgrounds** — the party screen shifts between dark (lobby, song) and light (ceremony, song selection)
- **Favicon / social share thumbnail**

**Logo should include:**
- A **wordmark** ("KARAMANIA") — stylized, not plain serif
- A **logomark** (icon/symbol) that works standalone at small sizes
- The mark should reference music/karaoke/party without being literal (avoid generic microphone-in-circle cliches)

**Ideas to explore:**
- A stylized microphone integrated into a letter (the "K" or "A")
- Sound waves / equalizer bars forming a crown or party hat
- A neon-sign aesthetic that evokes nightlife
- The musical note (already used in-app as the DJ icon) evolved into a brand mark

### 5.2 Color System

The app already has a sophisticated vibe-shifting color system. The logo and brand identity must work *with* it, not against it:

**Fixed tokens (brand foundation):**
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | `#0A0A0F` | Primary dark surface |
| Surface | `#1A1A2E` | Cards, elevated elements |
| Surface Elevated | `#252542` | Modals, active elements |
| Text Primary | `#F0F0F0` | Headings, body |
| Text Secondary | `#9494B0` | Labels, metadata |
| Action Confirm | `#4ADE80` | Success, awards |
| Action Danger | `#EF4444` | Errors, destructive |

**Vibe-shifting tokens (the logo must harmonize with all 5):**
| Vibe | Accent | Glow | BG | Primary |
|------|--------|------|-----|---------|
| General | `#FFD700` gold | `#FFD700` | `#1A0A2E` deep purple | `#6C63FF` indigo |
| K-Pop | `#FF0080` hot pink | `#FF69B4` | `#1A0A20` dark magenta | `#CC00FF` purple |
| Rock | `#FF4444` red | `#FF6600` orange | `#1A0A0A` dark red | `#CC4422` rust |
| Ballad | `#FF9966` peach | `#FFCC88` warm | `#1A1210` dark brown | `#CC8866` bronze |
| EDM | `#00FFC8` cyan | `#00C8FF` blue | `#0A1A1A` dark teal | `#00C8FF` electric blue |

**Recommendation:** The logo's primary rendering should use **gold (`#FFD700`) on dark (`#0A0A0F`)**  — this is the "General" vibe and serves as the default brand expression. The gold carries associations of stage lights, trophies, and spotlights. The logo should also render cleanly in white-on-dark and dark-on-white for flexibility.

### 5.3 Typography Direction

Current: The app uses system fonts with varied weights. The wordmark "KARAMANIA" is displayed in bold serif (`fontWeight: w700`).

**Recommendation:** The wordmark should use a custom or distinctive font that feels:
- **Slightly condensed** — fits "KARAMANIA" (10 chars) on one line at large size
- **Bold/heavy weight** — commands attention
- **Slightly stylized** — a touch of flair (subtle ligatures, custom letterforms) but still readable
- **All-caps friendly** — the brand name will almost always be rendered in caps

Avoid: script/cursive (too soft), pixel/retro (wrong era), ultra-thin (no presence on dark bg).

### 5.4 Iconography Style

The app uses **native emoji** for reactions and vibe-specific content (confetti, reactions). The brand iconography (navigation, UI controls) should complement this:
- **Rounded, friendly line icons** — not sharp/geometric
- **2px stroke weight** at standard size — visible against dark backgrounds
- **Filled variants** for active states

## 6. Competitive Positioning

| App | Visual Vibe | How Karamania Differs |
|-----|------------|----------------------|
| Smule | Clean, music-streaming feel | Karamania is more *party*, less *studio* |
| Karafun | Corporate/functional | Karamania is social-first, not catalog-first |
| Spotify (Wrapped) | Bold, graphic, editorial | Similar celebration energy — Karamania should match this for ceremonies |
| Jackbox Games | Playful, illustrated | Closest comp for "party game on phones" feel |

**Key differentiator:** Karamania is the **Jackbox of karaoke** — it's not about the music library, it's about the shared experience. The brand should feel more like a party game than a music app.

## 7. Deliverables Needed

1. **Logomark** — standalone icon, works at 48px-512px
2. **Wordmark** — stylized "KARAMANIA"
3. **Combined lockup** — mark + wordmark (horizontal and stacked)
4. **App icon** — logomark adapted for iOS/Android icon grid
5. **Color on dark** — primary rendering (gold on `#0A0A0F`)
6. **Monochrome variants** — white, black, for flexible contexts
